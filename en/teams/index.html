Start a Team

Teams are organized learning circle communities, typically affiliated with library systems and community-based organizations. While anyone can facilitate a learning circle for free, organizations that wish to build a thriving network of learning circles often benefit from additional support and tools.

Team membership is designed to provide comprehensive guidance via:

  • Staff Professional Development
  • 1:1 Support
  • Community-Powered OER Library
  • Software for Teams
  • Community of Practice
  • Discounted Additional Services

Team membership is offered on a sliding scale between $2,000 - $10,000 per year, calculated based on your organization's annual operating budget.

Staff Professional Development

Collaborate with the P2PU staff to deliver professional development tailored to your content and community needs:

  • Staff training focused on effective strategies for facilitating peer learning and the learning circle model
  • Quarterly consultations to set and pursue your organization’s program goals
  • Regular P2PU-facilitated virtual learning circles based on popular topics decided by member libraries

1:1 Support

Receive comprehensive guidance from the P2PU team to ensure staff feel supported and learning circles run smoothly and effectively:

  • Dedicated P2PU staff liaison and regular support hours for facilitators
  • Guidance for facilitators developing learning circles in new topics
  • Promotion and publicity for learning circles
  • Community platform for sharing practices from your library with the wider P2PU network

Community-Powered OER Library

Work with the P2PU staff to develop Open Educational Resources (OER), guided by your local community’s needs and shared with a global commons of learning circle facilitators:

  • 6+ P2PU facilitation guides each year (topics nominated by members)
  • Curated list of ready-to-use courses that align with your team’s and patrons’ preferences (2022)
  • Early access to new courses designed for learning circles

Software for Teams

Access a suite of tools to help you promote, manage, and evaluate your learning circle program:

  • Dedicated P2PU team page with active/archived learning circles, facilitator profiles, and info about your organization
  • Customizable participant surveys (2022)
  • Aggregated signup and exit survey data
  • Weekly insights on program activity
  • Collaborate with P2PU staff to shape the features and priorities for the Teams platform

Community of Practice

Join a global community of educators committed to P2PU’s innovative peer learning practices:

  • Two free tickets to our yearly community Gatherings (in-person, pandemic willing!)
  • Co-host a monthly public community call to share your program with the global learning circle community
  • Collaborate and connect with other P2PU Teams on projects of mutual interest, grant opportunities, and governance of the membership program.

Discounted Additional Services

Teams receive a 20% discount on all additional services, including course creation, workshops, and software development. Read more about our services.
